HIKE AT NORTH TABLE MOUNTAIN

Posted 10/30/2022

This summer I hiked at North Table Mountain and took this photo. The trail at the beginning is very steep, but the payoff of the view at the top is worth it! About 20 minutes of hiking with a couple breaks to catch your breath and you reach the top of North Table. Then, off to the right side there is a path to go a little bit further up for a picture of this cool cliff.

Know before you go:

  • Steep trail, exceeding 25% grading to get to the top
  • Rattlesnakes can be found in the area
  • Wear sunscreen, protective clothing, becuase there's no shade at the top

References: Check out the North Table Mountain Hiking page on the City Of Golden website
